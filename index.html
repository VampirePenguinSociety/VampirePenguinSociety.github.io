Vampire Penguin Society

A Casual Guild With Some Awesome People

The Vampire Penguin Society (VPS) is, at its core, a group of people who want to hang out online, play video games together, and have fun.

Most of the time, we play Elder Scrolls Online (ESO); however, that is far from the only game we play, with other notable games we often play being Don't Starve Together and Minecraft (Java Edition, aka the best edition).

Never heard of us before, but want to know more? Head on over to the About section to learn a bit more about the guild. If you're an ESO player, we also suggest checking out the Trials and Events sections.
