Progression Team

Vampire Penguin Society's trial progression team is the VPS Mudballers.

So far, the team has cleared all of the vCraglorns and the vCraglorn hard modes, and is now progressing through veteran Maw of Lorkhaj.

The team does accept new people when we have open spots; however, these spots are filled on an invite-only basis.

How to get invited, you ask? Well, running open trials with the guild is a good start. People who get invited are those who respect others, work well with the rest of the team, and listen to the trial host, among other things.
