Trial Requests

Is there a specific trial you want to do? You can request it using this form.

Request Guidelines

  1. Do not request vet-hm DLC trials. Generally, most of the guild is not ready for that at the current moment.
  2. If requesting a vet DLC trial, understand that it may not be cleared and will likely serve as more of a practice run.
  3. If you request a trial, make sure it is at a time you are able to join and a trial you want to do. Do not schedule on others' behalf -- the form is available to them, too.
  4. Don't try to schedule over an existing trial.
