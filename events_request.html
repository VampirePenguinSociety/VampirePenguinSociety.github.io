Mini-Event Requests

Is there a specific mini-event you want to do? You can request it using this form.

Request Guidelines

  1. Try to avoid requesting content that has a small group requirement, such as a trial or arena. Those are better scheduled in the #looking-for-group channel on the Discord.
  2. If you request a mini-event, make sure it is at a time you are able to join and an activity that you want to do. Do not schedule on others' behalf -- the form is available to them, too.
  3. Don't try to schedule over an existing event.
